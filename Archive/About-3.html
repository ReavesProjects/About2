ABOUT SHARON
Owner. Founder. Design Geek.

My friends used to joke that I started companies as an excuse to design new websites. Perhaps it was true as one of the happiest memories from my time as a gallery owner was waking up at 3am with an idea for a new exhibit. I'd rush to grab my laptop and draft out concepts on my website to see how works of art looked together.

I'm the person you mention in passing that you have an idea and the next morning I suprise you with a new site. God help my dentist or any site I might visit as I insist I can do it better. This is when I knew - perhaps I always knew - it was content and not art that I was meant to curate.
